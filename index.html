About Our Hotel

Situated a 10-minute walk from Deira Gold Souk and Banyas Square, this hotel features a 24-hour reception desk and modern rooms with a private balcony. A breakfast buffet is served every morning. A satellite TV and air conditioning are available in all rooms at The Royal Prince. Each is decorated with colourful art works and comes with a private bathroom. The restaurant serves buffet-style dinners in a tropical setting with wall photos of exotic beaches.

A number of restaurants and shops can also be found in the direct surroundings of Royal Prince Hotel. The hotel is located a 20-minute taxi drive from Dubai International Airport. Banyas Square Metro Station and Dubai Creek are both less then 700 metres away.

More About Us

Royal Prince Hotel – Premium Comfort in the Heart of Dubai

Nestled in the vibrant district of Deira, Royal Prince Hotel offers travelers a perfect blend of affordability, elegance, and convenience. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our hotel provides a peaceful retreat just minutes from the Gold Souk, Baniyas Square, and the Dubai Creek.

Guests can enjoy modern amenities including private balconies, 24/7 room service, high-speed Wi-Fi, and buffet dining. With easy access to metro stations and just 20 minutes from Dubai International Airport, Royal Prince Hotel ensures a seamless experience for every guest. Discover unmatched hospitality in the heart of the city.
